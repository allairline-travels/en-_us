🛫 Scoot Airlines Customer Service: Full Support Guide

Looking for help with your Scoot Airlines booking or flight? Here’s everything you need to know to get full customer service support — anytime, anywhere. 🌐

📞 24/7 Phone Helpline

Scoot offers round-the-clock phone support in several countries. Call the number that applies to your region:

  • 🇸🇬 Singapore: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🇮🇳 India: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🌎 Other countries: Dedicated lines available for Australia, China, Japan, Malaysia, and more.

💬 Live Chat on Website

Need instant help? Use the live chat feature available on the official Scoot website to chat with a support agent in real time. Great for help with flight changes, bookings, and questions. ⚡

📝 Submit an Online Inquiry

Scoot does not offer a direct email, but you can submit issues using their online contact form. Be sure to:

  • Choose the correct category
  • Include detailed information for a faster resolution

📲 Scoot Mobile App

Manage your booking, check-in, and get updates right from the Scoot app. It’s available for both Android and iOS devices. 📱

🛄 Airport Customer Service Desk

If you're at the airport, visit a Scoot service counter for urgent help with baggage or flight issues. 🚀

🌐 Social Media Support

Connect with Scoot on social media and send a message:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter

Expect a timely reply through direct messaging. 💬
